Download Osmo for Mac

Osmo runs on macOS 14 and later, on Apple Silicon. It installs outside the Mac App Store — that’s what lets it read your conversations locally and stay private.

Download Osmo (.zip)

Installing

Osmo is signed and notarized by Apple, so it opens like any other Mac app — no security warnings. Just unzip, drag Osmo to your Applications folder, and double-click.

Or install in one line

Prefer the terminal? This downloads Osmo and installs it to your Applications folder:

curl -fsSL https://leftonread.in/install.sh | bash

Once you open Osmo, it walks you through a summon shortcut and one Accessibility permission, with a plain reason for each.
